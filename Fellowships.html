Definition of the Target Audience & Scientific Projects

Scientific research is a progressive activity that begins at the undergraduate level (through introductory research internships), continues during the Master's degree (where substantial and sometimes original work may lead to publication), and extends to the PhD and postdoctoral levels.

At the PhD level, original research contributions are required, while postdoctoral researchers typically demonstrate increasing autonomy and scientific maturity.

Funding Policy

Funding is exclusively dedicated to human research activity and scientific output. No material costs (such as computers or equipment) are included.

Eligible expenses include:

  • Research fellowships and contracts
  • Conference participation (registration fees, travel, accommodation)

All research activities will be conducted at the University of Lisbon.
